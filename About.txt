Stealth Game is a game created by Dat Nguyen in 2017. It depicts a cat and mouse type of stealth gameplay where you have many tools to outmaneuver your enemy. The goal 
of the included map is to hack and steal data from a computer. The computer is located at the top in a central area. You will have to stay within the location in order to succeed hacking. Enemies will spawn in when you are
located.

Game Mechanics: You can crouch or hide in shadows to be hidden from enemies. The screen will turn black and white to indicate that you are hidden. 


Controls:
WASD to move
C to crouch
V to roll 
Left Shift to run
1 to throw a smoke bomb
f to throw a rock
Right Click to teleport to your orb.
